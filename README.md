## What is this?

     A simple script based on [RegalStreak](https://github.com/regalstreak/skadoosh) to compress android sources for users who have low bandwidth.

## Requirements

    Linux or Mac or WSL

## How to use?
   
    $ ./rom-compress.sh    
   
## How to extract?
    cat *.tar.xz* | tar -xvJf - -i
